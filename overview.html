Burmese doesn't have an official romanization. What we use daily is called Myanglish and it doesn't mark tones clearly. So, I decided to create my own romanization based on IPA and Myanglish.

I put * because
/ɹ/ is only for loanwords
th2 /d̪ð/ is very rare
