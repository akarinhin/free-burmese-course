Lesson 1 : Burmese Consonants

Every Burmese consonant has a creaky “a” sound. For example, if you see က, you read it as “ka”, not “k”. The names of the consonants are in the second line.

က
sound – ka
name – ka gyí
ခ
sound – kha
name – kha gwáy
ဂ
sound – ga
name – ga ngè
င
sound – nga
name – nga
စ
sound – sa
name – sa lóun
ဆ
sound – sa
name – sa lèin
same sound as စ
ဇ
sound – za
name – za gwé
ည
sound – nya
name – nya
အ
sound – a
name – a
transparent consonant
